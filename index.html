Our Ecosystem

Mutual Fund Distributor
A mutual fund is a financial vehicle that pools money from multiple investors to invest with a set objective. Once obscure, mutual funds are now widely used, often seen as a smarter alternative to idle cash in savings accounts.
Estate Planning
Estate planning is the process of arranging the transfer of assets to legal heirs during or after one’s lifetime. It ensures the distribution of property aligns with the estate owner's goals, especially in case of death or disability.
Non-Resident Indians
NRIs and OCBs can make portfolio investments in shares or debentures only through designated branches of authorised dealers, preferably near stock exchanges. These dealers must report branch names to the Reserve Bank and obtain approval.
Capital Markets
Capital markets facilitate the trading of financial securities like stocks and bonds, helping businesses raise funds. Venture Capital (VC) and Private Equity (PE) involve investing in companies.
